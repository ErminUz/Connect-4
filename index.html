Description

The game is played on a board which has seven hollow columns and rows.
The board consists of 42 windows made up of 6 rows and 7 columns.
Each player takes turns in placing their coins in chosen columns,
the coin will be placed in first empty container closest to the bottom of the board.
Both players start out with 21 coins, with individual colors.
The goal is for the players to connect their placed coins
so that they create a unbroken chain - diagonally, horizontally or vertically.
